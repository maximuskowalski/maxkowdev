Who I Am

My name is Matt, after finishing school as part of the very first Batemans Bay High School cohort in 1989 I went off to university to study architecture, industrial design, and art.

I have worked in advertising, marketing and graphic design for clients across a broad range of industries and more recently in fast turnaround print and digital media.

I'm a creative person who likes to think outside the box and solve problems. I have a passion for technology and design, and I'm currently working as a Software Engineer with the #100Devs Agency and taking on freelance work at maxKowDev.
